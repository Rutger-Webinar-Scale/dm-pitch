The 4 that matter most
Get these right and you're playing outbound on easy mode.
01
Volume
It stays a volume game. Always ask: how do I 5×–10× this? "How many did you send?"
02
Follow-up
3 for cold, up to 8 for engaged. Most replies land on follow-up #2–#5.
03
Results
Sell the vacation, not the flight. "I can get you clients." Never the details.
04
Offer
The biggest variable. An irresistible offer multiplies even weak copy.
